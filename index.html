Hello World -- I am Yingshi Feng!

As an artist, I focus on exploring the relationship between human and nature. I am the person who enjoys the nature and has inspired by environment a lot. But according to the time pass and human activities. Many issues come up, and our planet becomes worst. I am trying to address those problems in my work.

In the first art piece, "Time Travel," I tend to remind people of the joy during childhood, time is passing fast, and engage people to cherish what we have today. In the second art piece, "Teach Human Nature," I feel the technology developed very fast, human keep walking forward，it changes the relationship between human and nature, we should look for the balance. Another work "Steps," it expired the earth has changed since the human step in, especially after the industrial revolution, human activity has been a significant contributor to climate change.
